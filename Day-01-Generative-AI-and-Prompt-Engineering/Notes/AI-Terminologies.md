# AI Terminologies

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has many important concepts and terms. Understanding these terms helps in learning about AI, Generative AI, and Large Language Models (LLMs).

---

## Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI is the field of creating machines that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making.

---

## Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning is a branch of AI where systems learn patterns from data and improve their performance over time.

---

## Deep Learning (DL)

Deep Learning is a type of Machine Learning that uses neural networks to process large amounts of data.

---

## Generative AI

Generative AI is AI that can create new content such as text, images, audio, video, and code.

---

## Large Language Model (LLM)

An LLM is an AI model trained on large amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like language.

Examples:
- GPT
- Gemini
- Claude

---

## Neural Network

A Neural Network is a computing system inspired by the human brain that helps AI models learn patterns.

---

## Token

A Token is a small piece of text that an LLM processes. It can be a word, part of a word, or a character.

---

## Parameter

Parameters are values learned by an AI model during training that help it generate responses.

---

## Prompt

A Prompt is an instruction or question given to an AI model to generate an output.

Example:

```text
Explain AI in simple words.
```

---

## Prompt Engineering

Prompt Engineering is the process of creating effective prompts to get better results from AI models.

---

## Training Data

Training Data is the information used to train AI models, such as books, websites, and documents.

---

## Fine-Tuning

Fine-Tuning is adapting a pre-trained AI model for a specific task or purpose.

---

## Hallucination

AI Hallucination happens when an AI model generates incorrect or fictional information.

---

## Summary

These AI terms provide the foundation for understanding Generative AI, LLMs, and how modern AI systems work.
